Who We Are

FaithDefenders Aid (FDI) is a Nigerian non-governmental organisation (NGO) working with communities, government, and the private sector to promote spiritual, physical, and social wellbeing for all Nigerians.

We strengthen health systems and unify public and private efforts to deliver quality essential services nationwide.

Learn More

Our Projects

We implement projects in partnership with communities, donors, and government agencies to deliver better health outcomes across Nigeria through our office in Kano State Nigeria.
