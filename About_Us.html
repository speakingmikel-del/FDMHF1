About Us

Faith Defenders Missions & Humanitarian Foundation (FDMHF)

Faith Defenders Missions & Humanitarian Foundation (FDMHF) is a Christian non profit, and non political organization committed to defending the faith, empowering lives, and extending compassion to the vulnerable.

Founded on the conviction that faith must find expression through love, FDMHF stands at the intersection of spiritual growth, humanitarian service, and community transformation.

We reach believers in crisis, strengthen the faith of the oppressed, and equip communities with sustainable means of living. Through our diverse missions from relief response and empowerment to apologetics training, we exist to make Christ known in both word and deed.

Our Story

What began as a vision to strengthen believers in faith has grown into a mission touching lives across Nigeria and beyond.

From emergency response in conflict-affected areas to youth empowerment, faith training, and advocacy against gender-based violence, our story is one of compassion in action.

Every outreach, every training, every gift of support carries one message Christ still cares, and we are His hands extended to the world.

Our Vision

To see believers and communities strengthened in faith, restored in hope, and equipped to thrive spiritually, socially, and economically.

Our Mission

To defend the Christian faith through training and advocacy while providing compassionate aid, empowerment, and sustainable development to vulnerable individuals and communities.

Our Core Goals

  • To provide relief and empowerment through skill development and education.
  • To advocate for persecuted believers and raise awareness globally.
  • To foster partnerships for greater community impact.
  • To address physical, emotional, and spiritual needs for holistic growth.
  • To support the renovation and construction of places of worship.
